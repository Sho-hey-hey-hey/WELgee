Who is WELgee?

WELgee aims to create the society where shape the future together regardless of one’s circumstances
Over 70 million refugees are forced to escape their homeland due to conflicts, repressions, and worsening security.
Even in countries that have lost their homeland and fled, there are refugees here in Japan who have lost their future.
However, they are actually the same generation as us. Dreaming IT engineer, journalist full of sense of justice, gentle pastor ...
They are the treasure mountain with a unique personality.

"Anyone can play an active role if there is an opportunity"
      
       It ’s not our unilateral “support” but our future.

       Create a future where you can draw your second success.

WELgee's Main Activity

JobCopass

WELgee’s Job Copass program is for asylum-seekers living in Japan, who are seeking for their professional career and bright future in Japan.
The concept of Job Copass is simple, we collaborate together with you towards an empowering “job”. This means you will go step by step to be employed at a Japanese company and work in a field that suits your interests and specialty! We start by having meetings to design your career in Japan, doing training like Japanese language or computer programming, and we start thinking what kind of companies will be a good match for you!
After employment, we try to switch your visa from the Designated Activity visa for asylum-seekers to a professional visa for specialized workers.
Although there are many challenges along the way, with your passion and our collaboration, we believe that you will have alternative path towards your life goals.
